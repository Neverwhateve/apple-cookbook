## Naming Notes

- Prefer symptom-first names: device or feature plus the customer-visible failure.
- Keep one canonical recipe per repeated customer problem.
- Use `iPhone` for iPhone-first issues even when iPad or Apple Watch appears as a related device.
- Use `Networking` for Wi-Fi, cellular-adjacent network access, and Personal Hotspot issues that cross device families.
- Use Chinese feature categories only where the existing folder already uses Chinese naming, such as `查找` and `连续互通`.
