# Related Recipe Map

Use this index to maintain cross-links between canonical recipes. Links should help a Retail Specialist move quickly to the next likely branch, not create broad topic clusters.

## Strong Hubs

- iMessage, SIM, SOS, eSIM, and Wi-Fi recipes form the cellular/messaging troubleshooting cluster.
- Screen Time and Ask to Buy recipes form the Family Sharing controls cluster.
- Find My location sharing and AirPods Find My setup share location, account, and privacy boundaries.
- Battery, software update, charging, charging-pause, black-screen/no-power, Apple Watch charging, and iPhone unavailable recipes share backup, power, temperature, and service-intake decisions.
- Apple Pay add-card and Apple Pay terminal-payment recipes form the Wallet issuer/merchant/device split.
- iPad keyboard and iPad accessibility-state recipes form the iPad input/focus troubleshooting cluster.
- AirPods one-side audio, charging, pairing, firmware, and Find My setup recipes form the AirPods pairing/power/audio triage cluster.
- Activation, SIM/eSIM, Apple Account, passcode, and Activation Lock recipes form the setup/restore ownership cluster.
- Locked Notes, device passcode, Mac login password, Screen Time passcode, and Apple Account verification recipes form the customer-facing "forgot password" split.
- Mac camera and iPhone WeChat camera recipes should remain separate: one is Mac camera permissions/selection, the other is iPhone app-specific camera behavior.
